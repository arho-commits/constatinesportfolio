Services

Color Grading

About Me

The Editor Behind the Story

Hi, I'm Ojokoh Constantine — a video editor who lives for the rhythm of a perfect cut. I built my foundation through countless personal projects, experimenting until the pacing felt second nature. With a sharp eye for detail, a strong sense of timing, and a love for the craft, I work closely with clients to ensure every second of their video earns its place on screen.

While my personal portfolio is deep, I recently took my skills to the professional stage with Melanie Ohumu — CEO & Founder of Meue. I’m honest about being early in my professional journey, but I bring the hunger and precision of an editor who has already spent hundreds of hours behind the monitor.
